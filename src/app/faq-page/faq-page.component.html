ATTENTION ALL GYM GOERS! Do you struggle with deciding the number of sets / reps / weight to do for each exercise?
Are you tired of choosing how to vary your own workouts? Or is your gym routine just too boring?
Would you prefer to let FATE decide the goal for your workout?

Rand-O-Rep is a random number generator where you can spice up your workouts by doing a random number of Reps (per set) along with a random amount of Rest time between sets.
For example, right now you may decide that you want to do somewhere between 6 and 12 Bicep Curls. Using Rand-O-Rep you get a Magic Number of 8, and using Rand-O-Rest you get a Magic Number of 2 minutes! Now’s the time where you pick your own weight (because you know you best :) and aim to do any number of sets (3-5 suggested) of 8 Bicep Curls with 2 minutes of rest.
The point is to make these decisions for you, so you can customize your workout around them (being the number of sets and the weight you’re using). Works for both you and your gym buddy!
BONUS Rand-O-Relay: Attempt to do the number of reps within that time frame!

Enter the Rand-O-Rep.

The Rand-O-Rep is more than just for gym-goers – it is for anyone who wants to think a little less and have more fun in their life!
It likely can be scientifically suggested that a person’s tiredness is connected to the number of choices they make in a day.
Making more choices makes you more tired, while making less choices makes you less tired. This is why it is incredibly important to have a good routine and good habits – these are things you do everyday without having to think.
If you are someone who wants to be less tired throughout the day, and make confident choices that will have a positive effect on your life, then the Rand-O-Rep is here to help.

The Rand-O-Rep has a variety of uses by itself!
  • Pick a random number between 1 and 2 (flip a coin!)
  • Pick a random number between 1 and 6 (roll a standard die!)
  • Pick a random number betwen 1 and 20 (roll a DnD die!)
  • Pick a random number between 1 and 26 (pick a Powerball number!)
  • Pick a random number between 1 and 69 (pick a single Lottery number!)
The Rand-O-Repertoire can even be transformed!
  • Rand-O-Read - Read X pages today and in Y minutes write down everything you learned!
  • Rand-O-Reach - Spend Y minutes in each of X unique stretching poses!
  • Rand-O-Relax - Do X sessions of "Me-Time" (TV episodes, video games) while remembering to take Y minutes in between to look away from the screen and stay hydrated!
  • Rand-O-Reflect - Spend Y minutes meditating then list out X things you are grateful for in life!
