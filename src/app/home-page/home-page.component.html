Rand-O-Rep

  • Change Minimum --- Two Open Hands
  • Change Maximum --- Two Closed Hands
  • Increase Number --- Open Hand
  • Decrease Number --- Closed Hand
  • Confirm Number --- Hand Pointing
  • Generate Random Number --- One Open Hand, One Closed Hand
  • Generate Random Rest --- One Open Hand, One Pointing Hand

What makes our user experience truly unique is that you can perform actions and modify values with the use of hand gestures!
To use the application, begin by clicking the "Start" button to turn on your camera and be able to see your beautiful face. Then, refer to the key below the video screen to perform your desired action with a sequence of hand gestures.

For example, to increase the lower bound of the Rep Range, simply:
1.) Put Two Hands Up.
2.) Put a single Open Hand Up to raise the value (repeatedly raising your open hand up and down until reaching the desired value).
3.) Put up One Open Hand, One Closed Hand to confirm the value.
